Q:Diffrence between inheritance and polymorphism
Ans:inheritance is applied to classes , polymorphism applied to methods or functions
inheritance in which one class derives another class and derived class is known as child class and 
another class is known as parent class,
the general meaning for polymorphism is that 'multiple forms'
types of inheriitance are: 
1.single inheritance
2.multilevel inheritance
3.multiple inheritance - not supported by dart
4.hybrid inheritance
types of polymorphism are:
1.method overriding
2.methiod overloading - not supported by dart
